About Titos Foundation

Founded in 2025 by CEO Titus Kimari, Titos Foundation has been a beacon of hope and support for the people of Bibirioni Ward. Our mission is to provide sustainable development through education, health initiatives, sports programs, and social welfare projects. Over the years, we have impacted thousands of lives by fostering community empowerment and self-reliance.
